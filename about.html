About Our School üè´

Welcome to First Step Montessori, where we believe in nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning from the very beginning. Located in the heart of Mysore, we are dedicated to providing a safe, stimulating, and nurturing environment for your little ones.

Our school follows the Montessori method, which emphasizes independence, freedom within limits, and respect for a child's natural psychological, physical, and social development. We create an atmosphere where children can explore, discover, and learn at their own pace.

At First Step Montessori, we understand that every child is unique. Our experienced educators work closely with each child to help them reach their full potential while building confidence, creativity, and critical thinking skills.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic educational experience that nurtures the whole child - intellectually, emotionally, socially, and physically - while instilling values of respect, kindness, and lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To be a leading Montessori school that empowers children to become confident, independent, and compassionate individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Owner

With a passion for early childhood education and years of experience in the field, our founder established First Step Montessori with a vision to create a learning environment where children thrive.

Dedicated to the Montessori philosophy, our founder believes that education should be child-centered, allowing each child to discover their unique talents and interests. Through personalized attention and a nurturing approach, we ensure that every child receives the support they need to succeed.

Our commitment extends beyond academics - we focus on developing well-rounded individuals who are confident, curious, and compassionate. We believe that the foundation laid in these early years shapes the future of our children.

"Every child deserves a nurturing environment where they can grow, learn, and flourish. At First Step Montessori, we are committed to providing just that."
